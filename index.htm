NIU OFFERS:

NIU offers bachelors degree in streams of Engineering and Management.

BCA

Bachelor of Computer Application is another amazing IT program at NIU, that does not only offer you outstanding curriculum delivery but also ensures that you are well acquainted with latest technology and trend in the field of information and technology.Besides BCA curriculum, we also inculcate essential skills demanded by global software industry through interactive learning process.

BBM

BBM is a business and management degree that helps you excel your business acumen through industry focused curriculum. With a BBM degree, you can specialize in major in-demand areas including Banking and Finance, Sales and Marketing, Insurance and Risk Management, and Entrepreneurship and Enterprise Development..

BBA

BBA is a business degree that equips you with excellent managerial skills combined with practical organizational skills and experience. Corporate world has become fiercely competitive, and people with mediocre managerial expertise have no place anymore.

Bsc. CSIT

BSc. CSIT is a specialized computer science and IT program that aims to develop very capable and innovative IT professionals, and we at NIU are one of the best institutions to have produced hundreds of excellent IT graduates over past decade.
