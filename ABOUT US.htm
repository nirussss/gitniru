Welcome to Mitho Foods, the home of authentic Nepalese cuisine in Kathmandu. Our name means “delicious” in Nepali, and that is exactly what we aim to offer: a delicious and satisfying experience that will make you feel like you are in the right place.
At Mitho Foods, we use only the freshest and finest ingredients.Our menu features a variety of dishes.WE believe in the saying "Good food good mood" .We also cater to different dietary preferences, offering vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and halal options.
